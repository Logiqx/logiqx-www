  <h1>20 May 2002</h1>

  <table class="news">
    <tr>
      <td>
        <p>Quick release while I have a few minutes spare:</p>

        <p><a href="Tools/MAMEDiff/">MAMEDiff
        v1.13</a>:</p>

        <p>- Automatically detects and handles new resource
        ROMs.<br />
        - New resource ROMs are now included in the -t/-T
        output.</p>

        <p><a href="Tools/DatUtil/">DatUtil
        v1.20</a>:</p>

        <p>- New resource ROMs in MAME listinfo are automatically
        detected.<br />
        - ClrMamePro log and Nebula driver loaders now use the
        STORE_ROM<br />
        macro (this makes -g work correctly, amongst other
        things).<br />
        - Improved -m (fix merging) behaviour for resource
        ROMs.<br />
        - Nebula drivers now output the correct 'romof' for Neo-Geo
        clones.</p>
      </td>
    </tr>
  </table>
