Welcome to my arcade emulation page - home of my CMPro/ROMCenter arcade dats and other useful tools.

Click here for my current e-mail address but please do not use it to ask where you can find arcade ROMs.

Also, please do not query the validity of the CPS-2 dats. The process Raz and myself use is very thorough and there has never been a mistake. I get too many people reporting non-existent errors in it!
